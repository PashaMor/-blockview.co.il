Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information BlockView collects, why, who it is shared with and what rights you have. It applies to the blockview.co.il website, the mobile app and the agent CRM, and it is written in line with the Israeli Protection of Privacy Law, 5741-1981 and its regulations, and with the GDPR where it applies.

1. Who we are

BlockView operates a 3D real-estate map where people find apartments for sale and for rent. For the purposes of privacy law we are the controller of the information described below.

Privacy questions: privacy@blockview.co.il

2. What information we collect

We collect only what the Service needs in order to work:

  • Account details — your email address and password (the password is stored encrypted by our infrastructure provider and is not accessible to us). If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive only an account identifier and an email address;
  • Profile and preferences — profile picture (if you upload one, stored resized and compressed), language, light/dark mode, notification preference and a saved search filter;
  • Activity in the Service — properties you saved, buildings you follow and personal notes you wrote (notes are private and never shown to others);
  • Listings you published — property details, description, price, building address, photos and a virtual-tour link;
  • Enquiries you sent to a publisher — name, phone number and the content of the message;
  • Agent applications — full name, phone number, brokerage name, broker licence number, city of operation and a free-text note;
  • Technical data — our infrastructure providers record basic server logs such as IP address, browser type and request time, for security and operational purposes.

3. Why we use the information

The information is used only for the purposes below. We do not sell personal information and we do not pass it to external advertisers:

  • to provide the Service — showing the map, saving favourites, publishing listings and delivering enquiries to the publisher;
  • to verify identity and manage permissions — user, agent or administrator;
  • to review and approve listings before they appear on the map;
  • to secure the Service and prevent fraud, abuse and spam;
  • to improve the Service and fix faults;
  • to comply with legal obligations and to handle requests and disputes.

4. Legal basis for processing

Providing your information is voluntary. We process it because it is necessary to perform our agreement with you (running your account and the Service), for our legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention and improving the Service), to comply with a legal obligation, or on the basis of your consent — which you may withdraw at any time.

5. Who we share information with

Information is disclosed only to the following:

  • Infrastructure providers — storage, database and authentication services (Supabase) and website hosting (Vercel), which act on our behalf and are bound to protect the data;
  • Map services — loading the map sends a request to the map-tile provider, which may see your IP address. Map content is based on OpenStreetMap and its contributors;
  • Google or Apple — only if you chose to sign in with them;
  • The listing publisher — receives the enquiry details you sent them;
  • Authorities — where required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and the safety of users.

6. Local storage and cookies

We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. Your browser's local storage holds only operational items: your sign-in token, the interface language and the display mode.

We do use Google Analytics to measure aggregate usage — how many people visit, which pages they open, and whether they came from the website or the app. Ad personalisation and Google Signals are disabled, your IP address is anonymised, and we never send Google your email, your account id or the identity of any specific listing. For this purpose Google sets measurement cookies in your browser.

Analytics runs only with your agreement. On your first visit we ask; if you decline, the analytics script is never loaded and no measurement cookie is written. Clearing your browser's site data resets the choice and we will ask again.

You can clear this data in your browser settings; doing so will sign you out.

7. Information you publish yourself

An approved listing — including the building address, property photos and details — is shown publicly to all users of the Service. Do not include sensitive personal information you would not want to be visible.

Photos uploaded to a listing are kept in publicly readable storage, so anyone holding the direct link can view them even after the listing is removed from the map, until the file is deleted.

8. Information security

Traffic to the Service is encrypted (HTTPS). Access to data is restricted at the database level using row-level security policies, so each user sees only their own data and an agent sees only their own listings and enquiries.

Passwords are stored only as hashes. Administrator accounts require two-factor authentication, and agents can enable it themselves.

No system is completely immune. We will report a material security incident as required by law.

9. How long we keep information

Account information is kept while the account is active. After a deletion request we delete the account and the personal information linked to it, except information we must keep by law or to defend against claims.

Enquiries (leads) remain with the publisher who received them. Removed listings may be kept in an internal archive for a reasonable period for audit and fraud-prevention purposes.

10. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right:

  • to review the information we hold about you;
  • to request correction of information that is wrong, incomplete or out of date;
  • to request deletion of your account and your information;
  • to withdraw consent you gave, including consent to notifications and mailings;
  • to receive a copy of the information you provided, and to object to or request restriction of processing, where the GDPR applies to you.

11. How to exercise your rights

Most actions are available directly in the Service: editing your profile, changing your password, deleting favourites and notes, and removing listings.

For anything else, including account deletion, email privacy@blockview.co.il from the address registered to your account. We respond within 30 days.

12. Transfers outside Israel

Our infrastructure providers may store and process information on servers outside Israel, including in the European Union and the United States. Where that happens, we work to ensure the transfer complies with applicable law and uses accepted safeguards.

13. Children

The Service is not intended for anyone under 18 and we do not knowingly collect information about children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us and we will delete it.

14. Changes to this policy

We will update this policy from time to time. An updated version will be published on this page with a new update date. We will notify you of a material change through the Service or by email.

15. Contact us

Questions, requests or complaints about privacy: privacy@blockview.co.il

You also have the right to complain to the Israeli Privacy Protection Authority at the Ministry of Justice and, if the GDPR applies to you, to the supervisory authority in your country.
